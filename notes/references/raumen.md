# NOTES: "RAUMEN"

Japanese national food “Raumen”.<br/>
“Raumen” is a noodle soup loved by all Japanese. <br/>

## Four components of raumen:
1) noodles
2) sauce
3) broth
4) toppings

## Key ingredients in the sauce
1) salt
2) soy-sauce
3) miso

## History

> 拉麵傳入日本是從1871年簽訂「日清修好條約」開始

> 紛紛從大陸移住日本，幾乎都聚集在橫濱，神戶，長崎等，居留地

> 當時移住日本的中國人大多出身於廣東，福建省

Since Year 1900, Chinese cooks started to make Chinese noodles in all over Japan.<br/>
—> "Japanese people re-make those noodle for Japanese climate and favorite taste."

Year 1958, Raumen rapidly became popular among the Japanese people;<br/>
—> the first instant noodle in the world was sold<br/>
—> 1961, Miso Raumen; 1971 Cup noodle
